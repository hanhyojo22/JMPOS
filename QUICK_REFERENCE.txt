═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                          STEP-BY-STEP: CREATE STAFF
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Login as admin
2. Go to Staff tab (bottom right)
3. Tap + button (floating action button)
4. Fill in form:
   - Full Name: (employee name)
   - Username: (unique login - 3+ chars)
   - Email: (valid email format)
   - Password: (6+ characters)
5. Tap "Create" button
6. New staff can login with credentials

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                          STEP-BY-STEP: EDIT STAFF
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to Staff tab
2. Find staff member card
3. Tap menu (⋮) button on card
4. Tap "Edit"
5. Update name and/or email
6. Tap "Update" button

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                      STEP-BY-STEP: RESET STAFF PASSWORD
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to Staff tab
2. Find staff member card
3. Tap menu (⋮) button on card
4. Tap "Reset Password"
5. Enter new password (6+ chars)
6. Tap "Reset" button
7. Inform staff member of new password

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                          STEP-BY-STEP: DELETE STAFF
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to Staff tab
2. Find staff member card
3. Tap menu (⋮) button on card
4. Tap "Delete"
5. Confirm deletion
6. Staff member access immediately revoked
